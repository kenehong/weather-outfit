Recommendation

A (Fullscreen) — Best match for the core idea: "see character, understand weather." Easy to build, easy to manage Gemini images (character only, no scene backgrounds).

Hybrid A+D is also worth considering — A's layout + D's emotion expressions + gradient backgrounds. Keeps things simple while adding personality.
